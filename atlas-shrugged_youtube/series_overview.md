# Atlas Shrugged YouTube Series Overview

## Series Information

- **Course:** Atlas Shrugged by Ayn Rand
- **Total Episodes:** 30
- **Parts:** 3 (Non-Contradiction, Either-Or, A Is A)
- **Episode Duration:** 12-15 minutes each
- **Total Runtime:** ~7 hours
- **Voice:** Antoni (ElevenLabs)

---

| 10 | Wyatt's Torch | Ellis Wyatt burns his oil fields and vanishes |

## Key Characters

### Heroes (Producers)
- **Dagny Taggart** - VP Operations, Taggart Transcontinental
- **Hank Rearden** - Creator of Rearden Metal
- **John Galt** - The mysterious leader of the strike
- **Francisco d'Anconia** - Copper heir playing a deeper game
- **Ragnar Danneskjöld** - The philosopher-pirate

### Villains (Looters)
- **James Taggart** - Dagny's brother, political operator
- **Wesley Mouch** - Betrayer, government bureaucrat
- **Orren Boyle** - Incompetent steel competitor
- **Dr. Robert Stadler** - Genius without integrity

## Publishing Schedule Options

### Option 1: Weekly (7.5 months)
- 1 episode per week
- Best for audience retention
- Allows time for production polish

### Option 2: Bi-Weekly (3.75 months)
- 2 episodes per week (Tue/Fri)
- Faster completion
- Requires being ahead in production

### Option 3: Part-Based (6 months)
- Release each Part as a batch
- Part 1: 10 episodes in 2 weeks
- 1 month break
- Part 2: 10 episodes in 2 weeks
- 1 month break
- Part 3: 10 episodes in 2 weeks
