Chapter Analysis
Key Themes
1
Money as Virtue
Francisco's speech presents money as a tool of achievement and free exchange. Hostility to money is hostility to human accomplishment itself.
2
Intellectual Corruption
The intellectuals provide philosophical cover for looting by preaching that nothing can be known, man is worthless, and suffering is noble.
3
Social Masks
The party reveals the gap between people's social poses and their true values. Francisco drops his playboy mask to reveal unexpected depth.
4
Recognition
Rearden and Dagny recognize each other as kindred spirits—different from everyone else at the gathering.
