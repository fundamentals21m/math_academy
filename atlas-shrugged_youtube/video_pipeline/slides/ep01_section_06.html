Novel Structure
Three Parts of Atlas Shrugged
Part I
Non-Contradiction
A world full of contradictions that cannot be sustained. Capable people mysteriously disappear.
Part II
Either-Or
The conflict intensifies. Characters must choose: create value or consume it. Think or evade.
Part III
A Is A
The mystery is revealed. The philosophy of rational self-interest presented in full.
Each title reflects a principle of Aristotelian logic
