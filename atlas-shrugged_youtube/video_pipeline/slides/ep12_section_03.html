Two Paths to Power
Pull vs Merit
Merit
Earning your position through demonstrated ability. You rise because you can do the job better than others.
The best surgeon gets patients because she saves lives. The best engineer gets contracts because his bridges stand.
Pull
Getting your position through political connection. You rise because you know someone, or you've said the right things.
The connected surgeon gets patients regardless of skill. The connected engineer gets contracts regardless of competence.
When Pull Replaces Merit
Bridges collapse. Patients die. Quality disappears. But the aristocracy of pull doesn't care—they're insulated from consequences by their connections.
