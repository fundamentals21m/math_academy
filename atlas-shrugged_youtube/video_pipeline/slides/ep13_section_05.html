Episode 13 Summary
The Most Powerful Tool
They cannot match the producers in ability, so they attack through morality. They make excellence feel like sin, success feel like crime, achievement feel like exploitation.
But Some Producers Are Beginning to Question
They're asking questions that challenge the false premises they've been taught to accept:
"Why should I feel bad for being good?"
"Why should I apologize for my achievements?"
"Why should I sacrifice to those who contribute nothing?"
