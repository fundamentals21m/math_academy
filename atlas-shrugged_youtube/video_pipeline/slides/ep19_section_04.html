Episode 19 • The Meaning
What It Means
Pain
Not adding suffering to difficulty
Pain comes from resistance to reality. Accept reality, and pain dissolves.
Fear
Assessing threats rationally, responding appropriately
Fear comes from uncertainty about your ability to cope. Know your abilities, and fear dissolves.
Guilt
Judging yourself by rational standards
Guilt comes from accepting standards you don't believe in. Hold only to rational standards, and guilt dissolves.
