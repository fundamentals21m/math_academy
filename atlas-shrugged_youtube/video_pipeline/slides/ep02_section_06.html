Episode 2
Key Themes
01
Creation vs. Consumption
Rearden creates value; his family consumes it without gratitude. Producers versus looters.
02
The Nature of Achievement
Rearden's joy in creating is noble and proper, despite society's condemnation of "selfish" pursuits.
03
Guilt and Duty
The family exploits Rearden's sense of duty, using guilt to extract resources while giving nothing.
04
Material and Spiritual
The false dichotomy challenged—Rearden's metal is both physical achievement and spiritual triumph.
