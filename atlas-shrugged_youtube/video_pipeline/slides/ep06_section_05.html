The Philosophical Conflict
Contempt for Commerce
Scientists Like Stadler
Consider business vulgar. Claim to pursue truth for its own sake, untainted by material concerns.
Artists Like Eubank
Believe commercial success is proof of artistic failure. Money is dirty, profit is exploitation.
What They Don't Acknowledge
Every scientist, every artist, every intellectual depends on the products of commerce for their existence. The food they eat, the buildings they work in, the tools they use—all created by the businessmen they despise.
The non-commercial class is parasitic on the commercial. They cannot exist without producers. Yet they claim moral superiority over those who feed them.
