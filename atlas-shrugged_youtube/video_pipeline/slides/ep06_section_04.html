The Tragedy of Stadler
Dagny goes to confront Dr. Stadler, hoping this brilliant physicist will speak the truth about Rearden Metal. What she discovers is devastating.
Stadler admits privately that Rearden Metal is "a remarkable thing"—but he refuses to say so publicly.
He has traded his independence for security. By accepting government money, he has made himself an instrument of the looters. His contempt for the practical has destroyed his ability to be impractical.
