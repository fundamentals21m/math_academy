The Legislation
Equalization of Opportunity Bill
What It Mandates
Any business owner operating in multiple industries must divest from all but one.
Rearden, who owns steel mills and ore mines, must sell his mines.
The Claimed Purpose
Helping the "little guy"—giving smaller competitors a chance against industrial giants.
The Actual Effect
Strips a productive man of property he built through decades, transferring it to those who never created it.
This is the inversion of exploitation. The productive are forced to surrender their creations to the unproductive. Achievement is punished. Incompetence is rewarded.
