An Act of Defiance
Wyatt refuses to let the looters benefit from his work. If they want his oil, they can try to pump it from burning wells.

The producers can leave. The mind can go on strike. When they do, they will leave nothing behind for those who sought to enslave them.
A Mystery
Where did Wyatt go? Who recruited him? "Who is John Galt?" takes on new meaning. Someone is taking these men.
Part One ends with fire—and with questions that will drive the rest of the novel.
