Episode 18 • Two Kinds of Love
Two Visions of Love
Love as Sacrifice
You prove love by giving up what you want. The ideal partner needs you, can't survive without you. Love means carrying someone's burden.
Love as Admiration
You love someone for their virtues. The ideal partner inspires you, challenges you. Love adds to your life rather than subtracting.
One makes love a duty. The other makes it a reward.
