Character Study
The Conspirators
JT
James Taggart
Taggart Transcontinental
President who relies on political connections rather than competence. Resents his sister Dagny's abilities.
OB
Orren Boyle
Associated Steel
Owner of an inefficient company that cannot compete with Rearden Steel. Wants government to handicap his competitor.
WM
Wesley Mouch
Washington Lobbyist
Rearden's own lobbyist, secretly working against his employer. A double agent feeding information to the conspirators.
PL
Paul Larkin
"Friend" of Rearden
Pretends loyalty while plotting against him. The false friend who profits from betrayal.
Each shares a common trait: they cannot create value themselves,
so they must seize it from those who can.
