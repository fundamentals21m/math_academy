The Core Principle
What is the Sanction of the Victim?
Evil is powerless without the cooperation of the good.
Looters cannot function without producers to loot.
Parasites cannot survive without hosts to feed upon.
Rearden's Sanction
He grants his sanction every time he accepts guilt for his success. Every time he lets his family drain him. Every time he obeys a regulation he knows is unjust. He is the looters' strength—without his compliance, they have nothing.
The moment he withdraws his sanction, he is free. And they are powerless.
