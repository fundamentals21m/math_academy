Manufactured Shame
Guilt as Weapon
Are you successful?
You should feel guilty—others are not.
Are you intelligent?
You should feel guilty—others are not.
Are you productive?
You should feel guilty—others are not.
This guilt has no basis in wrongdoing. The producer has harmed no one. But he has been taught that his success itself is an offense against those who have not succeeded.
No threat is made. No force is used. The victim destroys himself through his own misplaced virtue.
