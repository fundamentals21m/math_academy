The Implications
First
The producers are not truly victims—they are collaborators. They choose to accept the guilt, obey the regulations, serve the system. They could choose differently.
Second

Liberation is possible at any moment. Not through revolution or violence, but through simple refusal. Stop producing for those who punish production.
This is the strike. The mind on strike.
