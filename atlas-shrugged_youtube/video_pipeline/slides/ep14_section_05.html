Episode 14 Summary
The Key to Atlas Shrugged
The looters rule only because the producers let them. The system of sacrifice continues only because the victims cooperate. The moment they withdraw their sanction, the entire structure collapses.
What John Galt Offers
Not a new ideology or political system, but a simple truth: you don't have to cooperate in your own destruction. Stop granting your sanction. Let the looters try to run the world without you.
