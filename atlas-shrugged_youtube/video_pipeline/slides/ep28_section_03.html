Episode 28 • What Selfishness Means
Two Kinds of Self-Interest
False Selfishness
Grabbing whatever you want without thought. The addict who ruins his health for pleasure. That's not self-interest—that's self-destruction.
Rational Selfishness
Identifying what actually serves your life—long term, comprehensively, realistically—and pursuing it with discipline and integrity.
The rational egoist is, paradoxically, the most reliable person you could meet.
