Francisco d'Anconia
The Money Speech
First Point
Tool of Exchange
Money doesn't create wealth—it represents wealth already created through productive effort.
Second Point
Source of Money
Money is made by the effort of every honest man who knows he can't consume more than he has produced.
Third Point
Barometer of Virtue
When money ceases to be the tool by which men deal with one another, men become the tools of men.
The Core Argument
If money is not the medium of exchange, then force must be.
Money represents voluntary trade; its abolition means the rule of force.
