## Posting Best Practices

### Optimal Times (US Eastern)
- **LinkedIn:** 7-8 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Avoid:** Weekends (lower engagement)

### Engagement Strategy
1. **First 30 minutes:** Respond to ALL comments
2. **Reply with questions:** Drive conversation
3. **Tag relevant connections:** CFOs, treasury managers, fund managers
4. **Share to relevant groups:** Bitcoin, Finance, Treasury Management

### Hashtag Strategy
**Primary (always use):**
- #Bitcoin
- #BitcoinForInstitutions
- #InstitutionalBitcoin

**Secondary (rotate):**
- #CorporateTreasury
- #BitcoinStrategy
- #BitcoinEducation
- #HardMoney
- #DigitalAssets

**Trending (add when relevant):**
- #MichaelSaylor
- #BlackRock
- #BitcoinETF
