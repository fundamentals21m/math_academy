FAS87 / FAS132 Problems
ERoA Assumption:
Companies assumed 8% return regardless of actual performance — direct credit to earnings
Smoothing Mechanism:
Bad years amortized over 15+ years, masking true impact of underperformance
CFO Incentives:
Bonuses derived from these earnings — obstinately unwilling to put them at risk
The Result:
Tail wagged the dog — equity-heavy strategies chosen to hit 8% regardless of risk
Just as FAS87 derailed pension reform, goodwill treatment discouraged bitcoin treasury adoption — until FASB's mark-to-market fix
