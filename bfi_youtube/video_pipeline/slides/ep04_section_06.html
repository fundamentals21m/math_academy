The ETF Tradeoff
What You Get vs. What You Risk
What ETF Provides
+ Easy exposure to bitcoin's price
+ No need to understand self-custody
+ Fits into existing brokerage accounts
+ Familiar investment vehicle
What ETF Costs
− Enormous counterparty risk
− No ability to verify custody yourself
− Dependence on custodian's competence
− Not true bitcoin ownership
The ETF captures bitcoin's price volatility — but at enormous counterparty risk. It is not a substitute for true bitcoin ownership.
