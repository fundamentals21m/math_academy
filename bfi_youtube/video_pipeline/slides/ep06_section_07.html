Episode 6 Recap
Key Takeaways
1
Bearer assets derive ownership from possession — whoever holds it owns it
2
Bitcoin is the most powerful bearer asset ever created — non-physical, easy to validate, impossible to seize
3
Physical bearer assets have prohibitive costs — gold requires military-level security
4
Cash lost bearer status in 1971 when Nixon closed the gold window
5
Self-custody enables unprecedented sovereignty — unlimited wealth in a 12-word seed
Institutions must understand: people will place a significant premium on bearer ownership — and apply a deep discount towards every layer of institutional ownership.
