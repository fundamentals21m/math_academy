The Borrowers
Two Types of Bitcoin Holders
The Degen
  • Accumulated bitcoin through reckless borrowing
  • Not afraid of debt or high yields
  • Terrible borrower by traditional standards
  • Views self as attacking the dollar
  • Will absolutely prioritize bitcoin loans
The Saver
  • Typically doesn't like debt, avoids it
  • Considered good risk to traditional lenders
  • Their bitcoin is everything to them
  • Will go to ends of world to protect stack
  • Should exhibit AAA-equivalent behavior
Both types will prioritize bitcoin-collateralized loans first
