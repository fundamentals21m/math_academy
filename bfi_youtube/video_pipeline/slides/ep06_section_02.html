What is a Bearer Asset?
Ownership Through Possession
Definition
A bearer asset is a financial asset where ownership is determined by physical possession, rather than by registration or the holder's identity.
Gold Standard Era
Cash = Bearer Asset
Cash represented a claim on Central Bank gold reserves. Whoever held it owned the claim.
