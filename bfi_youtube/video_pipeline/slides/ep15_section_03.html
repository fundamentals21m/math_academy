The Agency Problem
Inadequate Reporting
Asset-Only Framework
Managers only saw asset returns. Everything defined by "beating the S&P 500" — liabilities invisible.
The Misunderstanding
Beating S&P by 50 bps when it returns -20% doesn't help the pension at all. But they couldn't see this.
Career Risk
CFO telling CEO "pension is great" when bonds lost 30% — too much career risk, even if liabilities also dropped 30%.
Bitcoin Parallel
Without proper metrics, even obvious improvements get rejected due to how they "look" to leadership.
The Strategy (MSTR) Problem
The market doesn't know how to value MSTR because they don't know how to associate value with their bitcoin treasury. Same problem, new asset.
