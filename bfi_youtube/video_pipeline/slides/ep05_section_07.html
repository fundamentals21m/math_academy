Episode 5 Recap
Key Takeaways
1
Mastery takes time — like guitar, bitcoin requires years of dedicated practice
2
Bitcoin requires multi-disciplinary knowledge: economics, CS, history, math, game theory, cryptography
3
Companies think in quarters — but bitcoin mastery is closer to a lifetime than a quarter
4
Bitcoin expertise is scarce — arguably fewer than 5,000 people have institutional-grade mastery
5
The only advice: Start today. Beginning the journey now gets you there faster.
