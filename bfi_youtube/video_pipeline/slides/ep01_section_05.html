Key Insight
The Power of Capital
It is a misconception that the game is to build the biggest stack.
The game is to accumulate the most powerful form of capital.
Strong Holders
Those with earning potential and value-add capabilities have more optionality and power with their bitcoin.
Circular Economies
Bitcoin holders who build trading networks create more powerful capital than passive accumulators.
Institutional Limits
Even dedicated institutions like Strategy will act like individuals with limited power in the Bitcoin economy.
