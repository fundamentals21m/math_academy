The Path Forward
New Metrics Required
Institution Type New Metric Needed
Pensions Funded status or projected time to termination — not just asset returns
Corporate Balance Sheets Redefine strength: future value over present value
Structured Credit Redefine credit quality: bitcoin's impact on subordination
Asset Managers Track clients' overall allocation to bitcoin
The Warning
Without these new optics, institutional bitcoin efforts will be temporary — no more resilient to calamity than before their participation.
