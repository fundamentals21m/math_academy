Series Summary
Key Lessons
For Investors
  • Bitcoin is the greatest performing asset in history
  • Drawdowns are structural — always recover to ATH
  • Small allocations cause significant outperformance
  • Don't be unintentionally short bitcoin
For Fiduciaries
  • Liability exposure from NOT offering bitcoin
  • Drawdowns are short, returns are enduring
  • More likely to be sued for lack of returns than losses
For Executives
  • Don't need treasury bitcoin to benefit
  • NQDC and selling into rising markets are less risky
  • Lower operating costs without balance sheet risk
